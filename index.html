Excel Project

This is an Excel dashboard built from a sales dataset, using PivotTables to calculate core KPIs which includes: Revenue, Cost of Goods Sold, Profit, and Customer Count. The dashboard visualizes profitability by product to identify profitable products, revenue by sales rep to surface the strongest contributor to the revenue generated, and cost of goods sold by city to highlight cost differences across regions.

It also tracks monthly customer trends to flag the weakest performing month. Region and Category slicers let users filter the entire dashboard interactively, making it easy to drill into specific segments without rebuilding any charts.

Logistics Dashboard

This is a four-page Power BI dashboard built on a trucking and freight dataset, created as my capstone project for my TS Academy analytics certification. It covers driver performance, route profitability, fleet utilization, fuel efficiency, and safety metrics, pulling data across multiple related tables and using custom DAX measures to surface the metrics that actually matter to a logistics operation.

The goal was to showcase a report to spot underperforming routes, flag safety risks, and track fuel costs without digging through raw spreadsheets.

About

I am a geoscientist by education and a data analyst by training. My undergraduate degree gave me the foundation to work with complex data and make sense of it, and that is exactly what I do now, perhaps with different applications.

I studied Applied Geophysics at the University of Ilorin, where my coursework involved interpreting subsurface data including seismic readings, well logs, and similar datasets to understand what is happening beneath the earth's surface.

I completed my NYSC service year at Eko Electricity Distribution Company (EKEDP), where I worked across two units under the Technical Services department. In the Operation Technology unit, which is the company's GIS unit, I mapped company assets using ArcGIS Enterprise and the FieldMap app and worked on modifying the underlying asset database. In the Project unit, I supervised 3 projects and reported their status to my manager using tools like Excel and Power BI.

Since then I have continued building my skills. I completed a beginner to intermediate data analytics course with TS Academy covering Excel, Power BI, and SQL, with an introduction to Python. I am also currently working toward Microsoft's PL-900 certification.

Day to day I work with Excel and Power BI for analysis and dashboards visualization, and ArcGIS for spatial work which ties back naturally to my geoscience background. I am based in Lagos and looking for a graduate trainee or entry-level data analyst role where I can keep growing and contributing from day one.
